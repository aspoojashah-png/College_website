Admissions

Admissions to BMSCE are conducted through various entrance examinations such as KCET, COMEDK, PGCET, and GATE, depending on the program. The admission process is transparent and follows guidelines set by regulatory authorities. For undergraduate programs, candidates must have completed their 10+2 education with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as core subjects. Postgraduate admissions require relevant undergraduate qualifications along with valid entrance exam scores. The application process typically involves online registration, counseling, and document verification. The college provides clear guidance and support throughout the admission process to ensure a smooth experience for students. With a focus on merit and diversity, BMSCE attracts some of the brightest minds from across the country.
