About B.M.S College of Engineering

B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE), Bengaluru, established in 1946, is the first private e ngineering college in India, founded by Late Sri B. M. Sreenivasaiah and Late Sri B. S. Narayan. Approved by AICTE, affiliated to VTU, and autonomous since 2008, the institution is known for its excellence in engineering education and has produced over 40,000 graduates. BMSCE is accredited with an A++ grade by NAAC and its programs are NBA-accredited. It has achieved recognition through initiatives like TEQIP, GIAN, ARIIA rankings, and as a 5G use case laboratory. The college offers 17 undergraduate and 15 postgraduate programs, with strong research contributions and industry c ollaborations through centers of excellence and global MOUs. Known for its excellent infrastructure, innovation-driven learning, and outcome-based education, BMSCE ensures students are industry-ready through internships, projects, and training. With over 350 companies visiting annually for placements and highly qualified faculty, the institution continues to rank among the top engineering colleges in India
