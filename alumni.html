Alumni

BMSCE has a strong and widespread alumni network that plays a vital role in the growth and reputation of the institution. Graduates of BMSCE are placed in top multinational companies, research organizations, and entrepreneurial ventures across the globe. The alumni network actively contributes to the college through mentorship programs, guest lectures, internships, and placement support. Many alumni hold leadership positions in companies like Google, Microsoft, Infosys, and Amazon. Regular alumni meets and networking events help maintain strong connections between past and present students. Their success stories inspire current students and provide valuable industry insights. The legacy of BMSCE alumni reflects the institution’s commitment to excellence and long-term impact.
