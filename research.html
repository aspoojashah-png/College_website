Research at BMSCE

B.M.S. College of Engineering fosters a strong research culture that encourages innovation, problem-solving, and interdisciplinary collaboration. The institution houses multiple research centers across domains such as Artificial Intelligence, Robotics, Biotechnology, Civil Infrastructure, and Renewable Energy. Faculty members and students actively engage in funded research projects supported by government agencies like DST, DRDO, and industry partners. The college emphasizes publishing high-quality research in reputed journals such as IEEE, Springer, and Elsevier. Students are encouraged to participate in research activities from early semesters, promoting hands-on learning and critical thinking. Innovation and entrepreneurship are supported through incubation centers, where students can transform their ideas into startups. Workshops, conferences, and hackathons are regularly conducted to expose students to emerging technologies and real-world challenges, making BMSCE a hub for research excellence.
