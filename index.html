About the Principal

Dr. Bheemsha Arya currently serves as the Principal and Chairman of the Executive Council at B.M.S. College of Engineering (BMSCE), one of India's most prestigious private technical institutions. With an extensive academic background, including a B.E. in Industrial and Production Engineering from Bangalore University and an M.E. in Manufacturing Science and Engineering from U.V.C.E., Dr. Arya brings a wealth of expertise in mechanical and production engineering to his leadership role. Under his administration, the college continues to uphold its legacy of academic excellence and innovation, supported by Vice Principals Dr. L. Ravikumar (Academic) and Dr. Seshachalam D. (Administration). Beyond his internal responsibilities at BMSCE, Dr. Arya is a prominent figure in the wider educational landscape; he was recently nominated as a member of the 10th Council of Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU) for a three-year term starting in late 2025. This appointment highlights his influence in shaping technical education policies across Karnataka. Throughout his tenure, he has focused on fostering a research-oriented environment and strengthening industry-academia partnerships, ensuring that the institution remains at the forefront of engineering education and professional development

Founders

Late Sri. B.M.Sreenivasaiah

Founder B.M.S. Educational Trust

n the history of Karnataka, the name of Late Businayana Mukundadas Sreenivasaiah (BMS) occupies a prominent place in the field of Philanthropy. He started the B.M.S. College of Engineering in the same year.

Late Sri. B. S. Narayan

Donor Trustee B.M.S. Educational Trust

After the demise of "Sri. B.M.Sreenivasaiah", his dynamic and enterprising son Sri. B. S. Narayan, took over the reigns of the college. The institution grew from strength-to-strength under his able guidance.
