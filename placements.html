Placements

BMSCE has an excellent placement record, making it one of the top engineering colleges in India. The dedicated placement cell works closely with leading companies to provide students with career opportunities in various sectors. More than 300–350 companies visit the campus every year, including top recruiters like Microsoft, Amazon, Infosys, Wipro, and Accenture. Students receive offers in diverse roles such as software development, data analysis, core engineering, and consulting. The college provides comprehensive training programs including aptitude tests, technical skills, mock interviews, and resume building workshops. Internship opportunities further help students gain industry exposure before graduation. With strong industry connections and consistent placement performance, BMSCE ensures that students are well-prepared for successful careers.
